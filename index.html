About Me

Hello, I’m Owen! I am a graduate of Olin College of Engineering with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. I am passionate about space, renewable energy, and 3D printing and am currently working in manufacturing.

As the Mechanical Lead of the Olin Electric Formula SAE team, I guide my peers to create reliable, unique, and testable designs. My creative passion is pottery, specifically wheel throwing.

I’m also interested in cooking; my favorite creation will always be croissants because of the effort and technique required to make them.
